What is Indifferent Society, anyway?

Glad you asked :)

We're a relatively new outfit with a passion for live music and a love for our town's unique artistic culture.
We believe that Bloomington is home to some of the most fun and talented musicians around and are honored to be a part of the local scene.
Our group comes from all different backgrounds and our inspirations vary even more. We hope to reflect this in our style and in our music.
Above all else, we want to make people dance, have fun, and enjoy themselves.
It's great to be here now. ♥
